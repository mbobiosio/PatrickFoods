ABOUT AGROPARTNERSHIPS.‍

Agropartnerships is an innovative digital platform that gives everyone the opportunity to engage in Agribusiness anytime and on the go, by funding (investing in) farms and trading of agricultural commodities.

A core focus/key component of the platform is playing across the value chain of specific product types. Agropartnerships and its partners either Farmforte or trained smallholder farmers take seeds and nurture them into beautiful crops that produce a bountiful harvest, which is then processed and packaged into world-class products that are sold locally and across the globe.

By playing across the value chain, Agropartnerships and its partners take out the middlemen and provide consumers with the best quality at the best prices and still make excellent profits.‍
